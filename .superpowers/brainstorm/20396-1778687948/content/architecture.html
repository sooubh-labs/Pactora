Monetization Architecture

Proposed structure for AdMob and In-App Purchase integration

Core Services

AdService (lib/core/ads/)

Manages MobileAds SDK lifecycle. Lazy loads banner units. Global singleton.

IapService (lib/core/iap/)

Handles Google Play billing, purchase verification, and restoration.

PremiumProvider (Riverpod)

Reactively manages premium status. Persistent via Isar/Prefs. Controls ad visibility app-wide.

Ad Placement Strategy

A

List/Feed Injections

Every 5th item in Promise list, Timeline, and Archive.

B

Detail Screen Bottom Banner

Sticky banner at the bottom of Promise Detail and Person Detail.

C

Smart Upsell

Show "Go Premium" button in Settings and after X ads viewed.

Approaches

1

Standard Integration (Recommended)

Banner ads in feeds + Lifetime IAP to remove ads. Simple, clean, minimal UX impact.

2

Aggressive Monetization

Includes Interstitial ads on "Promise Created" + App Open ads. Higher revenue, higher friction.
